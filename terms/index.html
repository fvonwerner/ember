What Spare is

Spare helps you set a daily limit on apps you choose, keep a streak, and stay honest with a small circle of friends. It is a self-management tool: you configure it, and it acts only on your own device.

Using Spare

  • You need to be old enough to have your own Apple Account and to agree to these terms where you live.
  • Spare manages your own device only. Shields, limits, and unlock windows run locally; friends and partners can only answer requests you send — they never control your phone.
  • Circle is optional. Invitations use Apple's private sharing flow and must be accepted.
  • Be kind in your circle. Do not use display names, session titles, or request reasons to harass or impersonate someone. Spare may filter content, and users can report or block accounts.

Streaks, freezes, and honesty

Streaks, freezes, day outcomes, and phone-down time are motivational constructs computed by the app. A private iCloud recovery record may restore recent outcomes after reinstalling. A gap with a full unmeasured day can still break the streak, and iOS cannot recover usage from before the reinstall.

No professional advice

Spare supports habits around screen time. It is not medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice.
