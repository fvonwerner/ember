Screen time you keep, together.

Ember puts a daily limit on the apps you choose. Stay inside it, grow a streak, and let a few friends keep you honest.

Join the free beta on TestFlight

iPhone, iOS 26 or newer.

When time is up, it is up.

When today's budget is spent, your tracked apps close behind a gentle screen. There are exactly three ways forward, and every one of them is deliberate.

  • Close it

    Put the phone down and the day stays green. Your streak grows by one.

  • Take two emergency minutes

    A short wait, then two minutes in the app that matters. Up to three times per app each day.

  • Ask your lifeline

    One friend you choose can open a real exception: fifteen minutes, an hour, or the rest of the day. Days they helped stay calm blue on your calendar, never a broken streak.

A real limit, not a suggestion: there is no “keep scrolling anyway” button.

Private by construction.

Your screen time is measured by iOS and stays on your phone. Friends see your daily limit, your streak, and which days you kept. They never see your hours.

And the day begins at six in the morning, not midnight, so a fresh budget never lands in the middle of a late night.
